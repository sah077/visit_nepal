HERITAGE

Culture & Heritage

7 UNESCO sites, 125+ ethnic groups, and 3,000 years of living civilization

UNESCO World Heritage Sites

Kathmandu Durbar Square

Medieval palaces of the Malla kings — 50+ temples and courtyards in the valley's ancient hub.

Pashupatinath Temple

The holiest Hindu temple outside India — sacred to Lord Shiva, set on the banks of the Bagmati river.

Boudhanath Stupa

One of the world's largest Buddhist stupas, the spiritual center of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal.

Swayambhunath

The "Monkey Temple" atop a hill — 2,500 years old, revered by both Hindus and Buddhists.

Lumbini Sacred Garden

Birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama (Buddha) — sacred garden with monasteries from across the world.

Chitwan National Park

The first national park of Nepal — protecting Bengal tigers, one-horned rhinos and Gangetic dolphins.

Sagarmatha National Park

Home of Mount Everest — protecting the unique alpine ecosystem of the Khumbu region and its Sherpa culture.

Traditional Cuisine

🍚
Dal Bhat

Nepal's national dish — lentil soup with rice, curried vegetables and pickles. Refills are always free!

🥟
Momo

Beloved steamed or fried dumplings — stuffed with buffalo, chicken, or vegetables. Nepal's fast food.

🍜
Thukpa

Hearty noodle soup from the high Himalayan regions — warming and filling after a day's trek.

🫖
Butter Tea

Traditional Sherpa drink — tea churned with yak butter and salt. An acquired taste, but a cultural experience!

🍺
Tongba

Warm millet beer from Eastern Nepal — served in a bamboo vessel with a bamboo straw.

🍛
Kwati

Traditional Newari dish of nine mixed sprouted beans — served during Janai Purnima festival.

ESSENTIALS

Plan Your Nepal Trip

Everything you need to know before you go

🛂
Visa Information
Most nationalities get visa on arrival at Tribhuvan Airport. 15-day: $30 · 30-day: $50 · 90-day: $125. Indian nationals don't need a visa.
✈️
Getting There
Fly into Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM), Kathmandu. Direct flights from Delhi, Dubai, Bangkok, Doha and more.
💰
Currency
Nepalese Rupee (NPR). $1 ≈ 133 NPR. ATMs available in cities. Carry cash in mountain areas. Credit cards in tourist zones.
🌤️
Best Time to Visit
Oct–Nov: clearest skies, perfect for trekking. Mar–Apr: rhododendrons bloom. Dec–Feb: cold but peaceful. Avoid June–Aug monsoon.
📱
SIM Cards
Ncell and NTC SIM cards available at the airport. Buy a tourist SIM for $5–10 with data. Coverage in most trek routes.
🚌
Getting Around
Taxis, buses and tuk-tuks in cities. Domestic flights to Lukla (Everest) and Pokhara. Tourist buses between cities.
🏥
Health & Safety
No mandatory vaccines but Hep A, Typhoid and Tetanus recommended. Travel insurance with altitude evacuation cover is essential.
🆘
Emergency Contacts
Police: 100 · Ambulance: 102 · Tourist Police: 01-4247041 · HAMS Helicopter Rescue: +977-1-4255033
